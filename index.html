About Us


We are a group of professionals who love horses and nature. All of our instructors have years of experience and will guide students from beginner to master level. We provide private dressage and jumping lessons for adults and children. We also provide group lessons on request (maximum 10 people).

We do not have livery option at the moment but we are extending our stables and it is coming soon.

We are opened all year round !

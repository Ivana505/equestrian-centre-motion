Alexandra Schwartz - Dressage

Hi, I am Alexandra and I am the owner of the yard. With over 30 years of experience in dressage and horse training I will help you achieve your goals. Training horses and teaching people how to connect with the horse is my passion.

Dieter Fritz - Jumping

I will help you see your distance better, approach fence better and use all your aids at the right time. We will do ground work fundamentals to warm up and 20 meter circles. Classes are tense bu this will be adapted to horses age. All levels are welcome !

Samantha Donner - Dressage

We will focus on the hands, head and body posture. Soften your hands and remove tention to remove the stiffness. Using pilates excercises is a must as it will benefit you and give you better feel of your posture in the saddle.
(You can choose if you wish to leave stirrups or remove them before the class).

Markus Gebel - Jumping

Pace is the most important part of horse jumping. We will concentrate on getting the horse forward. I have developed special teaching techniques for kids 5-12 years which you can also find in my book "Pace for Ace!". Please contact us if you wish to puchase book.
